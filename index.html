There are over 35,000 homeless students enrolled in our L.A. County Schools.... It is in the A Blessing in a Bag Foudation's mission to uplift these children and help them succeed. We have to realize that in our economy making ends meet with little or no income can mean that parents have to compromise...Food or School Supplies?

Every $1.29 that you donate gives hope for healthy living through a bag of health necessities and heart felt items.

Each bag includes toothpaste, tooth brush, lotion, condoms, razors, shave cream, wipes, soap, floss, mouthwash, hand sanitizer, deodorant, feminine pads, pocket tissues, water.

ABB provides hygiene kits this summer, while fighting STD's, medical conditions and poverty on Skid Row (located in downtown Los Angeles, CA).
