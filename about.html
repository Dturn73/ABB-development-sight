Who Are We

Welcome to A Blessing in a Bag Foundation, Inc. official website. The specific purpose of A Blessing In A Bag Foundation is to improve the wellness of the community by providing supportive services for low to no income, homeless families and individuals. Thus, enabling these individuals to become self sufficient and productive, healthy members of society.

Our Mission is: Advocating, bonding and breaking barriers through supportive services for low to no income, homeless, troubled-age and at-risk youth, families and adults one community at a time.
